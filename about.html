A Historic Moment for Our Parish

St. Gregorios Jacobite Syriac Orthodox Church is under the patronage of His Holiness Moran Mor Ignatius Aphrem II (Prince Patriarch of Antioch & Supreme Head of the Universal Syriac Orthodox Church) and Mor Militheos Yuhanon (Patriarchal Vicar of Ireland). Fr. Joby Skaria is the founder Vicar of the Parish. The day to day affairs of the parish is managed by the Managing Committee elected by the Annual General body of the church.

St. Gregorios Jacobite Syriac Orthodox Church, the first parish of Syrian Orthodox Church in Ireland, was formed as per the bull of HG Dr. Kuriakose Mor Theophilose Metropolitan. Fr. Joby Skaria Celebrated First Holy Qurbono of this Parish on 23rd October 2005. Sajimon Cherian Koovappillil and Sunil Kurian were elected as the Trustees and Bibin George was elected as the Secretary on 1st January 2006.

We celebrate Holy Qurbono on every Sundays, we conduct Sunday school classes every Sundays. Morth Mariam Vanitha Samajam and Youth Association also meet regularly. Gregorian Choir adds to the beauty of worship. We conduct a Monthly Chain Prayer under the leadership of Vanitha Samajam while Youth Association helps to publish Gregorian Voice (Parish News Letter) and conduct Gregorian Study Forum.

Patriarch Of Antioch & All The East Moran Mor Ignatius Aphrem II, Catholicose of the East H. B. Aboon Mor Baselios Thomas I, Metropolitans Their Graces HG Mor Dioscoros Kuriakose, HG Mor Theophilose Kuriaksoe, HG Mor Ivanios Matthews, HG Mor Kuriakose Mor Dioscoros ,HG Mor Chrisostomos Markose and HG Mor Kuriakose Mor Eusebius have blessed our parish on various occasions.

By the abundant grace of God, St. Gregorios Jacobite Syriac Orthodox Church, Dublin has officially purchased its own church property a dream long cherished by our members. A plot extending to about 1.5 acres, along with a building of approximately 5,000 square feet, has been acquired to serve as the future home of our church and community. This evening, our Vicar Rev. FrJeny Andrews, together with Treasurer Mr. Binu B Anthinattu, Secretary Mr. Jiby Jacob, Managing Committee members and members of our parish, (St Gregorios Jacobite Syrian Orthodox Church Dublin Ireland )received the keys to the property. We extend our heartfelt thanks to our beloved Patriarchal Vicar, H.G. Thomas Mor Alexandrios Metropolitan for his unwavering support, prayers, and visionary leadership throughout this journey. This milestone is a testament to the prayers, unity, and deep faith of our parish community. We give thanks to our Lord and to all who supported and stood with us in love and dedication. May this new home be a sanctuary of faith, worship, and fellowship for generationstocome.
